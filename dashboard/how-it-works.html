🧠 How Does It Work?

Imagine having two really smart friends who help you understand Apple's money!

1

🗣️ You Ask a Question

First, you ask a question about Apple - like "Is Apple making more money?" or "Should I be worried about Apple?" Just like asking a question to a teacher!

2

👯 Two Smart Helpers Look for Answers

We have two helpers who look at Apple's information in different ways:

  • Helper A (Numbers Person): Looks at the exact numbers - like how much money Apple made, spent, and saved.
  • Helper B (Story Person): Reads the stories and explanations Apple writes about why things happened.
3

🔄 They Compare Notes

The helpers share what they found with each other. If they found different things, they keep looking until they find information they both agree on. It's like when two friends read different books about the same topic and then share what they learned!

4

✨ We Put It All Together

Finally, we combine what both helpers found into one clear answer. We also tell you how confident we are - if both helpers agreed, we're very confident! If they disagreed a lot, we tell you that too.

🎯 Three Ways We Give Answers

⭐

EXPLOIT Mode Best!

This is when both helpers totally agree! Like when two friends say the exact same thing. When this happens, we're really confident about our answer. We got this mode for the "operating performance" question.

⚖️

ADAPTIVE Mode Good

This is when the helpers mostly agree but see some things differently. We take the best parts from both answers. It's like combining two puzzle pieces that fit together!

🔍

EXPLORE Mode Careful

This is when the helpers found different things. Instead of guessing, we show you what both helpers found so you can decide! It's like when two friends have different opinions - both might be right in different ways.
